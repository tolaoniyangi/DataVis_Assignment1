    Tola Oniyangi

    I am a student at Columbia University's Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation (GSAPP) where I study both Architecture and Urban Planning. I work as a researcher at the university's Buell Center for the Study of American Architecture , doing architectural research for exhibitions, and an assistant in the Urban Planning program's Urban Analytics concentration, providing technical and administrative support to professors in the concentration.

    I am interested in intersections between architecture and technology, and have dabbled in web design, machine learning and mapping. I am excited to explore more forms of data visualization, and hopefully, I am able to combine my interests in design, planning and technology wherever I end up post-graduation.
